Aloha Rock Birds

Aloha Rock Birds is the outdoor decor idea inspired by the Hawaiian landscape. These unique, hand-made sculptors are meant to reflect the spirit of the Paradise Islands by use of ethnic colors and patterns.

Decor your garden

Aloha Rock Birds come in a variety of colors and sizes. They are a great way to decor your garden, and give it a unique, ethnic vibe.

About me

My name is Pola and I live in Kona, Hawaii. Before I moved to Hawaii from Poland, I have worked as a garden designer for over 20 years. Now I am trying to put together my experience in a field and my love for art. The result of this mix is Aloha Rock Birds: outdoor decor ideas inspired by Hawaiian landscape.
