Aloha Rock Birds Hawaiian Garden Decor

Aloha Rock Birds

Aloha Rock Birds is an outdoor decor idea inspired by the Hawaiian landscape. These unique, hand-made sculptors are meant to reflect the spirit of the Paradise Islands by use of ethnic colors and patterns. While we treat the material we work in with care and respect, our goal is to introduce elements of humor and fun into your garden.

Decor your garden

Aloha Rock Birds come in a variety of colors and sizes. They are a great way to decor your garden, and give it a unique, ethnic vibe. Whether you feel like adding some colors into your garden or thinking about a unique gift to someone dear, don't hesitate to contact us. We will be happy to provide you with customized sculptures, taking into account your individual needs and preferences.

About me

My name is Pola and I live in Kona, Hawaii. Before I moved to Hawaii from Poland, I have worked as a garden designer for over 20 years. Now I am trying to put together my experience in a field and my love for art. The result of this mix is Aloha Rock Birds: outdoor decor ideas inspired by Hawaiian nature.
